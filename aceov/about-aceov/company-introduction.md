Founded in London, UK, with its headquarters located in Colorado, USA, ACEOV is an innovative company specializing in **blockchain technology**, quantitative trading, AI algorithms, and digital financial services. Our business spans diversified areas, including asset custody, cloud services, digital payments, and crypto derivatives.

At present, ACEOV has established a presence in key markets across **North America, Europe, Asia, South America, and the Middle East**, becoming a trusted digital asset management partner for users worldwide.

Our **AI-powered quantitative trading system** leverages multi-factor strategies and machine learning models to capture market opportunities 24/7, dynamically adjust positions, and provide users with efficient, secure, and compliant digital asset trading and management services.

ACEOV is fully registered with **MSB compliance**, supports multi-currency and multi-strategy trading, and has a robust AI-driven risk management system, always prioritizing the interests of investors.

Our mission is: **to drive decisions with data and safeguard wealth with systems**. ACEOV is now recruiting community representatives and partners globally to jointly build a new ecosystem for decentralized finance!
