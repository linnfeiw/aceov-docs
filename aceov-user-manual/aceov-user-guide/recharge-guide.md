# 🇫🇰 Recharge Guide

1.On the homepage, click the Recharge icon.

2.Select XuPay.

3.Choose the currency type (USDT).

4.Select the network (TRC20).

5.Copy the deposit address and transfer funds from your exchange to the specified ACEOV address. Once the deposit is completed, the corresponding amount will be reflected in your account.

{% hint style="info" %}
The **minimum deposit** is **$50**.

During promotional periods, a deposit of **only $38** is enough to become an **active member**, easily unlocking **exclusive benefits**.
{% endhint %}
