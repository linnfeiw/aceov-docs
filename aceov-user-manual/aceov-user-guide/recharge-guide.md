1.On the homepage,click the Recharge icon.

2.Select XuPay.

3.Choose the currency&#x20;type (USDT).

4.Select the network&#x20;(TRC20).

5.Copy the deposit address and transfer funds from&#x20;your exchange to the specified ACEOV address.&#x20;Once the deposit is completed, the corresponding&#x20;amount will be reflected in your account.

{% hint style="info" %}
The **minimum deposit** is **$50**.

During promotional periods, a deposit of **only $38** is enough to become an **active member**, easily unlocking **exclusive benefits**.
{% endhint %}
